About Us

The taste of India is varied as its rich cultural tradition.
Every region has its own taste. There are mainly four regions in these Indian cooking styles; they are North, East, West and South Indian.
Joy Foods is an Authentic South Indian Restaurant.
We serve specialized menu from Kerala that includes selection daily soups, breads, meat, dosas, biryanis and vegetables, along with pickles, and specialties of the day.
Our restaurant serves the real South Indian food and thalis and the true flavors of home cooked food.
